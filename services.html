What We Offer

Comprehensive services for the Muslim community

We are committed to promoting Islamic education and knowledge development within our community. Our educational programs include Islamic studies and Quranic teachings, lectures on contemporary Islamic topics, community workshops and seminars, youth development programs, and knowledge circles and study groups. Education strengthens our community and helps us live according to Islamic principles.

Led by: Education Officer

üïå Worship Facilities

We provide dedicated spaces for prayer and worship, ensuring a peaceful and respectful environment for all our members. We offer prayer facilities for all five daily prayers, separate prayer areas for men and women, ablution (Wudu) facilities, Quranic recitation sessions, and special arrangements for Friday prayers and Islamic holidays. Worship is at the heart of our mission to strengthen faith and community bonds.

Led by: Spiritual Advisor

Zakat and charitable giving are pillars of Islam. Working together to ensure no one in our community is left behind.

We provide family-friendly recreational facilities and host a variety of community events in a halal and modest environment. Creating joyful memories while maintaining Islamic principles and family values.
