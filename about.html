Our Story

Founded in 2025, Daressalaam Islamic Club emerged from a vision to create a unique space where the Muslim community of Dar es Salaam could gather, celebrate, and find peace. Located along the beautiful shores of the Indian Ocean, our club offers the perfect blend of luxury, tranquility, and Islamic values. What started as a dream has grown into a thriving community hub.
