Membership & Enrollment

Members must be committed to Islamic principles and values. We welcome Muslim families and individuals of all backgrounds and nationalities. The membership application process is simple and typically takes 1-2 weeks for approval.

Simply contact us with your information, choose your membership plan, complete the application form, and make a payment. You'll receive your membership card and access details within 1-2 weeks.

Yes! You can upgrade to a higher membership plan at any time. If upgrading mid-year, you'll pay the difference. Downgrades can be made at annual renewal.

Annual memberships can be cancelled within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After that period, no refunds are provided, but membership can be paused for up to 3 months.
